### Checked-In Secret Detection: Strings are All You Need?

ZhengDong Huang, Kevin Li, Jinqiu Yang, Yepang Liu, Lili Wei.
**Co-First Author**
*ISSTA 2026*

An empirical study examining different secret detection techniques when applied to Android apps in APK format. The work analyzes the limitations of existing approaches and proposes new research directions.

### How Far Are App Secrets from Being Stolen? A Case Study on Android

Junfeng Chen, Kevin Li, Yifei Chen, Lili Wei, Yepang Liu
**Fourth Author**
*EMSE 2025.*

A systematic study characterizing app secret leakage issues in Android apps based on 575 potential app secrets, assessing their security impacts and revealing bad practices in app secret storage.

[Paper](https://scholar.google.ca/citations?view_op=view_citation&hl=en&user=CaDLpIwAAAAJ&citation_for_view=CaDLpIwAAAAJ:d1gkVwhDpl0C)
