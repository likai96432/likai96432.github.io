# Publications

## Selected Publications

### Automatically Detecting Checked-In Secrets in Android Apps: How Far Are We?

Kevin Li, et al.
**First Author**
*Submitted to EMSE 2025, under major revision.*

An empirical study examining different secret detection techniques when applied to Android apps in APK format. The work analyzes the limitations of existing approaches and proposes new research directions.

[Paper](https://scholar.google.ca/citations?view_op=view_citation&hl=en&user=CaDLpIwAAAAJ&citation_for_view=CaDLpIwAAAAJ:u-x6o8ySG0sC)

---

### Demystifying Device-Specific Compatibility Issues in Android Apps

Kevin Li, et al.
**Second Author**
*ICSME 2024.*

An empirical study examining the characteristics of device-specific compatibility issues in Android apps, with the goal of providing insights and directions for future research.

[Paper](https://scholar.google.ca/citations?view_op=view_citation&hl=en&user=CaDLpIwAAAAJ&citation_for_view=CaDLpIwAAAAJ:u5HHmVD_uO8C)

---

### How Far Are App Secrets from Being Stolen? A Case Study on Android

Kevin Li, et al.
**Fourth Author**
*EMSE 2025.*

A systematic study characterizing app secret leakage issues in Android apps based on 575 potential app secrets, assessing their security impacts and revealing bad practices in app secret storage.

[Paper](https://scholar.google.ca/citations?view_op=view_citation&hl=en&user=CaDLpIwAAAAJ&citation_for_view=CaDLpIwAAAAJ:d1gkVwhDpl0C)
